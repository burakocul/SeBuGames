Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Guess the hidden word in 6 tries. Green = right letter, right spot. Yellow = right letter, wrong spot. Gray = letter not in the word.

How often do new words appear?

A new word is available every hour, plus a special Daily Word that is the same for everyone each day.

How do I restore or sync my progress?

Progress is saved on your device. If you change devices or lose data, contact us at the email above and we'll help.

Which languages are supported?

Kelimecik supports 10+ languages including English, Turkish, German, French and Spanish.
